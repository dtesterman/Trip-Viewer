Boston, Plymouth & Newport

May 24–29, 2027 · Darrell + wife · 5 nights · Edward Doty Heritage Trip

📅 Best alignment: Arriving Saturday May 24 puts Plymouth on Monday (Memorial Day — Plimoth Patuxet open) and Wednesday for the return (Pilgrim Hall Museum is Wed–Sun only). Club Wyndham Long Wharf gets 3 consecutive nights (Mon–Wed) to meet the minimum points booking requirement. No rental car needed in Boston Days 1–2.

Day 1

Arrive Boston

Saturday, May 24 · Wyndham Boston Beacon Hill (cash)

✈️
Afternoon Fly into Boston Logan (BOS) Est. $25–35 taxi

Taxi or Lyft from Logan to hotel, ~15 min. No rental car needed in Boston.

🏨
3:00 PM Check in — Wyndham Boston Beacon Hill Est. $189–249/night

200 Stuart St, Back Bay. Standard Wyndham hotel (cash rate). Walking distance to Boston Common and Public Garden.

🍽️
Evening North End dinner Est. $60–90

Walk or short Uber to Hanover St — Boston's Little Italy. Giacomo's, Neptune Oyster, or Trattoria Il Panino. Skip the clam chowder — Italian is the play here.

After dinner Walk the Harborwalk Free

Stroll along the waterfront from the North End toward Long Wharf. Harbor views at sunset.

💡 If you arrive early enough, walk through the Boston Public Garden to see the swan boats and the Make Way for Ducklings statues.

Day 2

Boston — Trolley & History Day

Sunday, May 25 · Wyndham Boston Beacon Hill (night 2 of 2)

🚎
9:00 AM Old Town Trolley + Tea Party Combo $77/pp confirmed

Hop-on/hop-off trolley covers 12+ stops along the Freedom Trail: Faneuil Hall, North End, Beacon Hill, Back Bay, Bunker Hill. Includes Tea Party Museum and harbor cruise.

🫖
11:00 AM Boston Tea Party Ships & Museum Included in combo

Interactive experience — throw tea overboard, meet costumed actors, see the Robinson Half Chest (one of only two original tea chests).

🍽️
12:30 PM Lunch — Faneuil Hall / Quincy Market Est. $30–50

Historic food hall with dozens of vendors. Good variety beyond seafood — steak tips, pasta, Greek, barbecue.

⚓
1:30 PM USS Constitution & Museum Free

"Old Ironsides" — oldest commissioned warship still afloat. Active-duty Navy sailors give tours. Adjacent museum has interactive exhibits.

🚢
3:00 PM Harbor Cruise (included in combo) Included in combo

45-minute narrated harbor cruise. Skyline views, harbor islands, and Bunker Hill Monument from the water.

🍽️
Evening Dinner — Back Bay or Seaport Est. $60–90

Explore the Seaport District's newer restaurants or classic Newbury Street dining.

💡 The trolley runs every 15–20 minutes. Ride the full loop once without getting off (~100 min), then hop on/off at your favorite stops the rest of the day.

Day 3

Larz Anderson → Plymouth → Newport

Monday, May 26 · Club Wyndham Long Wharf (night 1 of 3 — points)

🚗
8:30 AM Check out, pick up rental car Est. $55–70/day

Enterprise downtown Boston. Begin drive south through Brookline.

🏎️
9:30 AM Larz Anderson Auto Museum $12/pp confirmed

"America's Oldest Car Collection." Housed in a 1927 carriage house. Rotating exhibits with pre-war automobiles, antique motorcycles. ~1 hour visit.

🚗
11:00 AM Drive to Plymouth

~50 min via MA-3 S through the South Shore.

🍽️
12:00 PM Lunch in Plymouth Est. $30–50

Waterfront dining — Blue Blinds Bakery (cafe fare), East Bay Grille (upscale casual).

⛵
1:00 PM Mayflower II & Plimoth Patuxet $42/pp confirmed

Combo ticket. Full-scale Mayflower replica with costumed interpreters portraying actual passengers. Plimoth Patuxet recreates the 1627 Pilgrim Village. Edward Doty was among the 102 passengers.

🏛️
3:30 PM Pilgrim Hall Museum $15/pp confirmed

America's oldest continuously operating museum (since 1824). Original Pilgrim artifacts including furniture, weapons, and the sword of Miles Standish.

🚗
4:30 PM Drive to Newport, RI

~1 hr 20 min via I-195 W and RI-24 S. Cross the Mount Hope Bridge.

🏨
6:00 PM Check in — Club Wyndham Long Wharf Resort Timeshare points (free)

142 Long Wharf, Newport. Waterfront resort. 3 consecutive nights on points. Kitchen-equipped suites.

💡 Monday is Memorial Day — Plimoth Patuxet will be open but expect heavier crowds. Arriving mid-afternoon helps you dodge the worst of it.

Day 4

Newport Mansions & Car Museum

Tuesday, May 27 · Club Wyndham Long Wharf (night 2 of 3)

🏛️
9:00 AM The Breakers $29/pp confirmed

Cornelius Vanderbilt II's 70-room Italian Renaissance palazzo. Newport's #1 attraction. Self-guided audio tour, ~1.5 hrs.

🏛️
11:00 AM Marble House $18/pp combo confirmed

William K. Vanderbilt's gift to his wife — modeled after Versailles. 500,000 cubic feet of marble. Adjacent to The Breakers on Bellevue Ave.

🍽️
12:30 PM Lunch — Thames Street Est. $30–50

Newport's main waterfront strip. Diego's (Mexican), Brick Alley Pub (burgers/pub), or Salvation Cafe (eclectic).

🏎️
2:00 PM Newport Car Museum $20/pp confirmed

85+ vehicles across 80,000 sq ft in Portsmouth (~15 min drive). Muscle cars, Corvettes, exotics, and rarities in a modern gallery.

🌊
4:30 PM Cliff Walk (partial) Free

3.5-mile coastal path behind the mansions. Walk the scenic first mile south from Forty Steps — ocean on one side, mansion grounds on the other.

🍽️
Evening Dinner — Bowen's Wharf Est. $60–90

Waterfront dining. The Mooring (harbor views), Clarke Cooke House (upscale), or 22 Bowen's (steak & wine).

💡 Buy the Newport Mansions combo ticket online in advance — saves 15–20% vs. individual admission and lets you skip the ticket line.

Day 5

Scenic Drives & Lighthouse

Wednesday, May 28 · Club Wyndham Long Wharf (night 3 of 3)

🗼
9:00 AM Beavertail Lighthouse & State Park Free

Conanicut Island (Jamestown), ~30 min from Newport. Dramatic rocky coastline, historic lighthouse (third oldest in North America), and a small museum.

🚗
10:30 AM Conanicut Island scenic loop Free

Loop around Jamestown — Fort Wetherill overlook, village streets, harbor views. Quintessential New England coastal charm.

🍽️
12:00 PM Lunch — Jamestown or Wickford Est. $25–45

Village of Wickford — charming waterfront with shops and cafes.

🚗
1:30 PM Rhode Island Farm Coast drive Free

Scenic loop through Saunderstown, Narragansett, and South Kingstown. Farm stands, stone walls, rolling fields meeting the ocean. This is the scenic-drive highlight of the trip.

🌊
3:30 PM Narragansett Town Beach & The Towers Free

Walk the seawall, see the iconic Towers arch. Optional ice cream stop.

🚗
5:00 PM Return to Newport

Back via scenic Ocean Drive or Route 138 across the bridges.

🍽️
Evening Farewell dinner in Newport Est. $80–120

Splurge-worthy: Castle Hill Inn (sunset views), The Chanler at Cliff Walk (fine dining), or Forty 1 North (harbor-front). Book ahead.

💡 This is the scenic-drive day. Beavertail at low tide reveals tidal pools; bring good walking shoes for the rocky shoreline.

Day 6

Depart — Plymouth Heritage

Thursday, May 29 · Check out day

🏨
9:00 AM Check out of Club Wyndham Long Wharf

Load the car, head north toward Plymouth for final heritage stops.

11:30 AM Mayflower Society House $10/pp confirmed

Headquarters of the General Society of Mayflower Descendants. Research library, historic house tours, and genealogy resources.

🍽️
12:30 PM Lunch in Plymouth Est. $25–45

Last New England meal before heading to the airport.

🚗
2:00 PM Drive to Boston Logan (BOS)

~50 min via MA-3 N. Return rental car at the airport. Allow 2 hours before your flight.

✈️
4:00 PM+ Depart Boston

Fly home. End of a great heritage trip.

💡 If your flight is later in the day, Plymouth Rock is a 2-minute walk from the waterfront restaurants — worth a quick look.
